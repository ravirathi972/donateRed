Steps to follow

Now calling for help is easy with us! Just follow 3 simple steps and book your ambulance at the urgent hour at your doorstep.

Enter your Location

Go to the button "Need help? Book yours!" at the Home page. The button asks you to enter your location and uses the address to find the nearest available hospitals and free ambulances. The charges will be shown.

Book your ambulance

To book your ambulance from the available list, pick the hospital suited to you. And click "Book Now" button. Your ambulance is booked. It will reach to you in 10-15 minutes.
